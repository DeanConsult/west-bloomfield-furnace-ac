Our HVAC Services

Residential and commercial heating and cooling services for West Bloomfield MI and the greater Metro Detroit area.

Furnace Repair

Our trained professionals at West Bloomfield Furnace and AC are certified to service all brands and models of furnaces. Furnace repair is one of our specialties and we have over 15 years of experience in this area.

Don't delay having your furnace repaired. A minor furnace problem today could become a major breakdown tomorrow. Our crews will come out within 24–48 hours to diagnose your furnace repair needs and give you an honest estimate.

Furnace Installation

A new furnace is a major purchase. Ideally, the decision to replace your furnace is made before you're stuck in a panic looking for emergency service. However you find yourself shopping for a new furnace, you want to be sure the installation is done right.

We offer many options for every budget. Ask us about high-efficiency models and the energy tax credits and rebates available — they can save you more than you might expect.

Air Conditioner Repair

If your AC isn't keeping you comfortable, don't put off getting the necessary repairs. Delay can mean more costly problems down the line. Our professionals are trained to service any make or model of air conditioner.

Many AC repairs are quick and inexpensive — a small freon leak, a faulty capacitor, or a simple replacement part. No matter what your needs, call us to discuss your AC repair — we consider ourselves experts and we will treat you right.

Air Conditioner Installation

Michigan summers are hot. You don't want to be caught without the comfort of central air conditioning. We offer a variety of AC units for all budget needs — and you'll enjoy quality cooling comfort for years to come.

Our professional installers are trained to get the job done right. Ask us about high-efficiency models and the energy tax credits and rebates available on qualifying equipment.

24/7 Emergency Service

We know how important your safety and comfort is. We are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for whenever your heating and cooling needs arise — nights, weekends, and holidays included.

Whether it's a broken furnace in the middle of winter or an AC failure on the hottest day of the year, West Bloomfield Furnace and AC is prepared to meet all your emergency HVAC needs. Call us now — we're ready.
