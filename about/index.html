Our Story
15+ Years of Trusted HVAC Service

At West Bloomfield Furnace and AC, we have over 15 years of experience working in the HVAC industry. We have built a great reputation in the West Bloomfield area and can service most of the Metro Detroit market. We serve both residential and commercial customers and offer 24-hour emergency service for those who need help right now.

Because of our reputation, most of the work we do comes from repeat customers or referrals from our current customers — and that tells us we must be doing something right.

All of our technicians have many years of experience and undergo continued education on new innovations and techniques in the HVAC industry. It is our goal to provide you with the best HVAC products and services on the market at competitive prices, because doing great work for a fair price is the key to keeping customers for life.
